#####Relative and absolute:
-If an element has position set to "absolute" it will position itself to the closest parent that has 
positions "absolute". By default, and if no parent has position "absolute" the element with
will position itself to the body without following the normal flow of the page.

-If an element has position "relative" it will position itself to its nearest parent and it will 
follow the normal flow of the page without disturbing it.

#####Margin and padding:
-Every element is contained inside a box made of 4 borders, top right bottom and left. the space between
the element and the walls of the box is called padding, and the space between the walls of the box and 
the elements outside the box is called margin.
